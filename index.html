Frequently Asked Questions

Scaffolding businesses are prone to accidents, slips, and other risks. Scaffolding insurance protects your business from legal expenses, claims, and ensures uninterrupted operations by preparing you for unforeseen uncertainties.

Liability for scaffolding can fall on:

  • Scaffolding installer or manufacturer: Faulty scaffolding-related incidents.
  • Property owners: Negligence in construction work arrangements.
  • Employees from other companies: Damages or injuries caused on-site.
  • Supervisors/contractors: Failing to ensure a safe working environment.

Recommended insurances for scaffolding businesses:

  • Public liability insurance
  • Contract works insurance
  • Income protection insurance
  • Tools, equipment, and stock insurance
  • Professional indemnity insurance

Our brokers can guide you to find the best fit for your needs.
